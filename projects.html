APEX Stent Project — AI‑Assisted Biodegradable Stent Research

The APEX Stent Project pioneers the integration of AI into biodegradable coronary stent research. As project lead, responsibilities include:

  • Design and implementation of a research database for imaging and experimental data.
  • Development and deployment of AI models for automatic SEM/TEM image analysis (YOLO-based pipelines).
  • Automated reporting: generating annotated diagnostic overlays and structured reports for corrosion, calcification, and thrombosis assessment.
  • Coordinating experimental validation with in vivo models and clinical partners.
